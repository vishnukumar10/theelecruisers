            <h2>Achievements</h2>
            <p class="desc">Our Profile</p>
         </div>

         <div class="col three-fourths">
             <p class="intro">We got the opportunity to present our research to <b>Mr. Avinash Chander</b>, the then <b>Director General, DRDO</b> who was extremely inspired by our work and also gave us a project which we are currently working on.</p>

             <p class="intro"><b>Savior UAV</b> was shortlisted for the <b>European Satellite Navigation Competition 2013.</b></p>

<p class="intro">We secured the <b>First Position</b> in Lighter than Air Vehicle Challenge Student Design Competition organized by <b>American Society of Mechanical Engineers</b> held at BITS, Pilani.</p>

<p class="intro">We have showcased our projects at the <b>Second Unmanned Systems Conference and Exhibition - 2014</b> held at New Delhi.</p>

<p class="intro">We were the only student team at <b>India Aviation Expo 2013 and 2016</b>, Hyderabad and were greatly appreciated by the delegates.</p>

<p class="intro"><b>National Aerospace Laboratories</b>, Bangalore has shown keen interest in two of our projects.</p>

<p class="intro">Our research has been printed in <b>The New Indian Express, Deccan Chronicle, Shanghai Express, Career 360</b> etc.</p>

<p class="intro">Our projects have been covered by <b>Vasanth TV</b>, a TV Channel.</p>

<p class="intro">We had exhibited our UAVs at <b>Maker Fest 2015</b>, held in Ahmedabad in January 2015.</p>

<p class="intro">Secured world rank 38 in <b>Students Unmanned Aerial Systems (SUAS)</b> Competition, held in Maryland, USA in June 2015.</p>

<p class="intro">Showcased our UAVs to <b>Mr. Ratan Tata</b>, Chairman Emeritus of TATA Sons, who was highly impressed with our on-going projects.</p>
