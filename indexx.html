ABOUT US

The team was established on the 14th of March 2016 by Mr. Mohammed Imran,Btech(Mechatronics) with support from
Dr. C. Muthamizhchelvan, Director (E&T) under the guidance of Prof. S.Rajasekaran of Department of Mechanical Engineering. The team was initially headed by Mr. Muffadal Bohra , class of 2017, B.Tech(Automobile).

DR. C. MUTHAMIZHCHELVAN

Director, Engineering and Technology

Prof. S. RAJASEKHARAN

Deputy HOD, Mechanical Department

The Elecruisers is formed by a group of 25 students from various Engineering disciplines like Automobile ,Mechanical, Mechatronics , Electrical and Compuer Science Engineering.The team is formed by the support of Department of Mechanical Engineering SRM University Chennai. The Elecruisers is the first team representing SRM University in the eBAJA SAEINDIA 2017.Our team was formed by the support of Department of Mechanical Engineering SRM University, Kattankulathur,Chennai. We represent SRM University in the eBAJA SAEINDIA events. eBAJA is an intercollegiate competition specific to off road racing. Our team designs, builds and assembles the vehicle. 

WHAT WE DO

We are a group of 25 automobile fanatics from SRM University, Chennai. We promote electric mobility for future generations. The Elecruisers is an organization which aims at bringing out and sharpening the skills of the engineering students from different fields. Our team comprises of students from fields of electronics, automobile, mechanical, electrical and mechatronics. We strive to put in all potential and fulfill our dream of securing top places in the event through various ideas and innovations.
